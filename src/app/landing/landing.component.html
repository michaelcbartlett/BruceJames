What is Marco?

Marco is a spatial placement plugin that helps you push sounds back in your mix while keeping their clarity and character intact. Perfect for realistic depth, cinematic space, and clean, intentional separation.

  • ✅ Frequency-aware placement
  • ✅ Match perceived energy and focus
  • ✅ Crossover-linked processing for realism
  • ✅ Clean, intuitive XY interface
