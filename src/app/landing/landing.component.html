Sounds like snake oil. It isn’t.

Phase, explained like a human.

“Fix your phase automatically” sounds like audio snake oil. I get it. But phase cancellation is real, measurable physics, and DeepPerfection attacks it in a way no other plugin does. Here’s the whole idea in three steps.

01

Two sources share the lows

A kick and a bass both live in the sub frequencies. Down there their waveforms overlap and interact, that’s a phase relationship.

02

The overlap cancels

Where the two waves oppose, they cancel, and that energy is just gone. Your low end thins out and you don’t know why.

03

It restores what’s lost

Route your kick to the sidechain once. DeepPerfection works out exactly what cancelled and additively rebuilds the low end you lost with no timing shifts, no pitch warping, zero new artifacts.

But the real win isn’t the correction. It’s that you stop thinking about phase at all. Throw a kick and an 808 together, layer subs, stack samples with abandon, and DeepPerfection keeps the low end aligned while you focus on the music.
