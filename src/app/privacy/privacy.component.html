Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

The short version: we collect as little as possible, we never sell your data, and analytics only runs if you say yes. Here are the details.

Who we are

brucejames.studio is operated by BruceJames, an independent audio plugin and music project based in Raleigh, North Carolina, USA. For anything privacy related, email {{ supportEmail }}.

Analytics (only with your consent)

If you accept the cookie banner, we load Google Tag Manager and Google Analytics to see which pages get visited and roughly where visitors come from. This sets cookies and processes your IP address and browser information. Google LLC (USA) processes this data on our behalf. If you decline, or simply ignore the banner, none of this loads and no analytics cookies are set.

You can change your mind at any time using the cookie settings at the bottom of this page.

Newsletter

If you join the plugin list, your email address is stored by Mailchimp (Intuit Inc., USA), which we use to send the emails. We only use it to tell you about new plugins, betas, and sales. Every email has an unsubscribe link, and unsubscribing removes you from the list. We never share or sell the list.

Purchases

Plugins are sold through Gumroad (Gumroad, Inc., USA), which acts as the merchant of record. Gumroad handles payment, billing details, and applicable taxes under its own privacy policy. From Gumroad we receive your email address and order details so we can issue your license key and provide support.

License management

When you activate a plugin or use the license manager on this site, we process your email address, license key, and a machine fingerprint (a non-reversible identifier for your computer, plus an optional name you give it). We use this to enforce activation limits and let you manage your own activations. This data is kept for as long as your license is active.

Hosting and server logs

This site is hosted on Vercel (Vercel Inc., USA). Like almost every web host, Vercel keeps short-lived server logs that include IP addresses, used for security and to keep the site running. Our license API is hosted at api.brucejames.studio and keeps similar logs.

Legal bases (for visitors in the EU/EEA and UK)

Analytics and the newsletter run on your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR), which you can withdraw at any time. Purchases and license management are processed to fulfil our contract with you (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR). Server logs rely on our legitimate interest in keeping the site secure (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR). Data is processed in the USA; where GDPR applies, our providers rely on safeguards such as the EU-US Data Privacy Framework or standard contractual clauses.

Your rights

You can ask us what data we hold about you, ask us to correct or delete it, and withdraw any consent you have given. EU/EEA and UK visitors also have the right to complain to their local data protection authority. To exercise any of these, email {{ supportEmail }} and we'll sort it out.
